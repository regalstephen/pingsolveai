TouchBattle PVP

Keep Your Touch. Outlast Everyone.

One finger. Don't lift. Last player standing wins.

TouchBattle is a real-time, touch-based multiplayer game that tests reflexes and endurance. Keep your finger on the screen, slide to move, chase shapes, and outlast everyone else.

How it works

  • Golden rule: Keep your finger on the screen. Lift and you're out.
  • Two ways to compete: High Score — collect shapes and avoid obstacles. Endurance — survive longer than everyone else.
  • Three ways to play: Practice solo, Private Match with a lobby code, or Public Match with players worldwide.

Climb the ranks

Earn XP and move through tiers from Bronze to Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, and Legend. Compete on leaderboards and see where you stand.

Make it yours

Unlock trails and emojis as you rank up. Premium unlocks all styles and removes ads so you can focus on the battle.

Jump in with Practice and Public matches. When you're ready, upgrade once to remove ads and unlock every style—no subscription.
