Privacy Policy for ChairGrab

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ChairGrab collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. For the most up to date version of this policy, please visit chairgrab.com/chairgrab-privacy.html.

1. Introduction & Scope

ChairGrab ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ChairGrab platform. This policy applies to all users of our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number) when you create an account or listing.
  • Listing information including salon name, address, and pricing details.
  • Communications between users on our platform.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Device information and cookies for platform functionality.

Third-Party Sources:

We may receive information from third-party services like Google if you choose to sign up using those platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To process transactions and manage user accounts.
  • To facilitate communication between space owners and beauty professionals.
  • To improve platform performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who assist in our operations, or when required by law to protect our rights or the safety of others.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, update, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal data.

9. Disclaimers & Limitation of Liability

While we strive to protect your data, ChairGrab is not liable for data breaches beyond our reasonable control.

10. Links to Other Websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates will be posted with a new "Effective Date." Continued use of the site after changes indicates acceptance.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding your privacy, contact us at: support@chairgrab.com
