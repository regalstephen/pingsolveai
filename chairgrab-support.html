Common Questions

How do I find a chair or suite?
Use the search bar on ChairGrab.com to find the right spot by service, amenities, or location. Check out photos, descriptions, and pricing. Once you find a fit, click on the listing and contact the salon owner directly.
How do I list my space?
Click the "List Your Chair" button on the top right of the site. You can chat with our AI assistant, Chairry, who will help you build your listing, or you can do it yourself. It's fast, flexible, and effortless.
Is there a fee to list my space?
Listers may post available salon chairs and spaces for a non-refundable listing fee. The fee amount is clearly stated prior to purchase.
Do I need an account to post a space?
No account or subscription is required to post your space initially. However, creating an account allows you to manage your listings from your profile screen.
Does ChairGrab handle the rental agreements?
ChairGrab is a listing and advertising platform only. We provide the space for Listers and Renters to connect. We are not a party to any rental agreements or transactions between users.
Who is ChairGrab for?
ChairGrab is for beauty professionals (stylists, barbers, nail techs, makeup artists) looking for space, and salon owners with unused chairs or suites looking to generate extra income.
